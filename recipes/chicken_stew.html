Chicken Stew

Description

This is a classic homestyle dish of Chicken Stew, containg all the nutrients and tastyness one would expect getting at home

Taking only 60 minutes to prepare, and serving the whole family, this dish is sure to be a winner!

Ingredients

  • 15ml vegetable oil
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cloves, crushed garlic
  • 45ml plain flour
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 400g (1 can) chopped tomatoes
  • 30ml cream of chicken soup
  • 6 chicken pieces, skinless

Steps

  1. In a large pot heat the oil, add the carrots, onion and garlic and fry on a gentle heat for 5 minutes
  2. Add the chicken pieces and fry until well browned on all sides
  3. Add the flour and stir so it makesa paste around the vegetables and chicken
  4. Gradually add the water and tinned tomatoes and bring to the boil
  5. Reduce the heat, cover and allow to simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked
  6. Mix the cream of chicken soup with 4 tablespoons of water to make a smooth paste. Add it to the pot and simmer for 2 minutes, stirring continuously, until thickened
  7. Serve the meal with brown rice for a more wholesome dinner
