Amarula Dalgona Coffee Cocktail

Description

The Amarula Dalgona Coffee Cocktail is a delightful fusion of South African cream liquer and a trendy coffee twist. Imagine a smooth layer of Amarula, with its rich velvety creaminess and hint of marula fruit, poured over ice in a chilled glass. Topped with a dollop of whipped coffee foam, this cocktail combines the earthy, caramel notes of Amarula with the bittersweet allure of coffee, creating a luxurious and balanced blend. Perfectly creamy, a touch fruity, and satisfyingly bold, this cocktail is an indulgent as it is refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 100ml Amarula Ethiopian Coffee Cream Liquer
  • 350ml milk
  • 6tbsp instant coffee
  • 4tbsp sugar
  • 6tbsp boiling water

Steps

  1. Whisk the coffee, sugar and boiling water in a bowl for approximately 5 minutes until the mixture is thick and fluffy with stiff peaks.
  2. Add ice to glass
  3. Pour milk and Amarula Ethiopian Coffee Liquer over ice
  4. Spoon over Dalgona coffee mix to layer
